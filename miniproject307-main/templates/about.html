About Us

With economic conditions, the number of cars increases and there is also a rise in sales of used cars. It is difficult for customers to estimate the price of a used car. Only with the help of experts can predictions for car prices be achieved. The experts' predictions were not enough to identify the fair price of cars.
While predicting the price of used cars, it depends on entirely different features and factors. Most significant features are selling price, model of car and also fuel type. The most popular ingredient for a car is type of fuel and the volume of fuel it consumes for each mile. It is also considered due to its frequent price changes.Aims to develop a machine learning model that can accurately predict the price of a used car based on its features, in order to make informed purchases by customers.
